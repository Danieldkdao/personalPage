Our Business

My brother Lawrence and I won first place in the 2025 Douglas County Youth Entrepreneurship Challenge with our business, “Don’t Panic AI Tutors,” which offers tutoring and workshops to help students understand AI, spark curiosity, and build the skills they’ll need in a tech-driven world. Our mission is to make AI education fun, accessible, and empowering. We later competed at the 2025 Kansas Entrepreneurship Challenge, hosted by Network Kansas, where we received the Exemplary Award — the highest honor at the competition. That recognition inspired us to keep growing our project.
